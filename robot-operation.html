Robot Operation

Imagine the Operation board game but with teleoperation

For my embedded systems and rapid prototyping class (CS 3651), a friend and I ended up building a little robot arm to play the board game Operation! To see it in action check out this video.

This project happened over the span of 2 months, involving lots of 3D printing, lasercutting, and playing around with circuits.
